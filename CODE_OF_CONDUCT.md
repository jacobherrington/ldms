# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not individuals.
- Use inclusive language.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insults, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing community standards.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to maintainers through the security contact process in `SECURITY.md`.
